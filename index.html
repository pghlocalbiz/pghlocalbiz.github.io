Senior Special is a pop-up program designed to connect senior residents with local business. Business owners can tak into a market they may otherwise lack, and seniors can feel welcomed and benefit from the services offered by their local business environment.

About section

We are a team of students at Carnegie Mellon University. As apart of our project for a course entitled "Design and Policy for Humanitarian Impact" we created a popup program for business owners and senior residents. After spending some time in Allentown, a neighborhood located in Pittsburgh, we ultimately discovered that there was a disconnect between businesses and the residents they serve. To mitigate this, we designed a popup program for business owners to conduct in a local senior center. Our program engages both parties, providing mutual benefit between them. Throughout this site, you can find information about our team, a guide in hosting a pop-up, and completed pop-ups and their results. We encourage you to share your results, and use this site as a guide in engaging an otherwise ignored population in your local business.
